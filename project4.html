Process Control System with Built-In Simulator

During my senior year at the University of Arizona, I developed a MATLAB-based control system to regulate the water level in a cylindrical tank. The system read pressure data from a transducer and adjusted an outlet valve using a motorized actuator. I built the entire system from the ground up, including a user interface with real-time adjustable settings, diagnostic feedback, and a built-in simulator with tunable parameters.
